
Key Do's
1. Maintain Complete and Optimized Profiles: Ensure all profile information is filled, include a clear picture or brand logo, and use a recognizable handle to improve credibility and engagement.


2. Create Valuable Content: Share content that entertains, informs, or inspires rather than just promoting products. Aim for an 80/20 ratio—80% valuable content and 20% promotional content . Use visuals like images, videos, and infographics for higher engagement .
   
3. Engage with Your Audience: Respond promptly to comments, messages, and questions. Ask questions, encourage discussion, and acknowledge user-generated content to foster two-way communication.


4. Be Authentic and Positive: Maintain a consistent voice aligned with your brand, transparently share sponsored content, and avoid aggressive or offensive remarks. Authenticity builds trust and long-term relationships 


5. Focus on the Right Platforms: Prioritize networks most relevant to your audience or business—LinkedIn for professional B2B engagements, Instagram and TikTok for visual and younger demographics, Facebook for community connection.

Common Don’ts
1. Avoid Oversharing Personal Information: Refrain from posting exact location, home addresses, or identifiable personal data 

2. Don’t Spam or Overpost: Avoid repeated promotional posts, excessive hashtags, or pruning irrelevant followers seeking numbers over engagement 


3. Don’t Automate Everything: While scheduling is okay, full automation can reduce authenticity. Personal interaction is key 
.

4. Avoid Offensive, Controversial, or Misinformed Content: Refrain from sharing fake news, offensive posts, or controversial opinions aiming solely for engagement 


5. Don’t Compare Yourself Constantly: Focus on your unique strategy and goals rather than replicating competitors, which can dilute your brand identity
